## **About the team + role**
We are building an elite team, applying frontier technologies to the world's biggest financial problems. We're looking for bold thinkers. Sharp problem-solvers. Builders who are wired to make an impact. Robinhood isn't a place for complacency, it's where ambitious people do the best work of their careers. We're a high-performing, fast-moving team with ethics at the center of everything we do. Expectations are high, and so are the rewards.
The Reliability Engineering team at Robinhood is focused on ensuring the scalability, performance, and resilience of the systems powering our millions of users. As a Staff Software Engineer, you'll be part of the founding team shaping our reliability practices from the ground up. Our mission is to build a culture and foundation of reliability into all areas of the stack--from brokerage and crypto to backend infrastructure and tooling. The team is also going to be building with other teams, specifically building out the pre-production/staging environments, working with observability, and internal developer tooling groups as well!
As a Staff Software Engineer, you will lead major technical initiatives and influence reliability engineering strategy across the company. You'll build and scale critical systems, work closely with partners across product and infrastructure teams, and be a core contributor to establishing standards and best practices for observability, testing, and system readiness. You'll operate with significant autonomy while mentoring others and setting a high bar for engineering excellence!
